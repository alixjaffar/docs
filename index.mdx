## What is Avallon?

Avallon is an AI-powered website builder that transforms your ideas into fully functional websites in seconds. Whether you're building a portfolio, business site, or e-commerce store, Avallon handles the design, code, and deployment automatically.

## Key Features

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Instant Website Generation">
    Describe your website in plain English, and Avallon generates a complete, responsive website with modern design, optimized code, and all necessary pages.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Visual Click-to-Edit">
    Don't like something? Just click on any element to edit text, swap images, and adjust styling in real-time.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="AI Assist">
    Use natural language to modify your website. Ask for changes like "make it darker" or "add a testimonial section" and watch it happen instantly.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Website Import">
    Import existing websites with full CSS, images, and multi-page support. Perfect for migrating or redesigning existing sites.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Custom Domains">
    Connect your own domain name with simple DNS configuration. Host your site on your own domain.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Quick Start

Get started with Avallon in three simple steps:

1. **Sign Up** - Create your free account at [avallon.ca](https://avallon.ca)
2. **Describe Your Site** - Tell Avallon what you want to build
3. **Edit & Publish** - Customize with the visual editor and publish instantly
